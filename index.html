Welcome to Our Website

The Home Builders Association of the Grand Traverse Area, Inc. is a professional association of industry leaders who support
legislative, educational and economic initiatives to promote home ownership in our community. The Association was chartered in 1970
and represents the five counties of Antrim, Benzie, Kalkaska, Leelanau and Grand Traverse.
